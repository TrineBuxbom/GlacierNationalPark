Welcome to Glacier National Park

A place where rugged mountains meet crystal-clear lakes, and nature still feels truly wild. Located in northwestern Montana, Glacier National Park is home to breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and over 700 miles of trails to explore.

Whether you’re here to hike, drive the famous Going-to-the-Sun Road, spot wildlife, or simply take in the views, Glacier offers something unforgettable in every season. This site will help you plan your visit, discover must-see spots, and make the most of your time in one of America’s most stunning national parks.
